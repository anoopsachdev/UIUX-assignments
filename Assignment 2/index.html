Travel Tips for Tokyo

  • Buy a Suica card for easy Metro travel.
  • Visit popular districts: Shibuya, Akihabara, Asakusa, and Shinjuku.
  • Try local food: Ramen, Sushi, Takoyaki.
  • Carry cash — many small shops don’t accept cards.
  • Learn simple phrases like “Arigato” and “Sumimasen”.
